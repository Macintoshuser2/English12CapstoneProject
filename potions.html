Modifiers:

Redstone - Extends how long the potion effect lasts

Glowstone - Enhances the level of the potion. (i.e. turns Strength into Strength II)

Fermented Spider Eye - Corrupts the Effect

Gunpowder - Turns a normal potion into a throwable splash potion.

Dragon's Breath - Turns a splah potion into a lingering potion

Potion of Regeneration



Regeneration potions Regenerate your health for a short period of time. (Made with a ghast tear and an awkward potion)

Potion of Swiftness



Swiftness potions make you walk and run faster for a short period of time. (Made with sugar and an awkward potion)

Fire Resistance



Fire Resistance potions make you invulnerable to fire and lava for a short period. (Made with Magma Cream and an awkward potion)

Potion of Healing



Instant Health potions give you an instant health boost for a short period of time. (Made with a glistering melon and an awkward potion)

Potion of Night Vision



Night Vision potions allow you to see clearly in the dark for a short period of time. (Made with a golden carrot and an awkward potion)

Potion of Strength



Strength potions increase the amount of attack damage you do for a short period of time. (Made with blaze powder and an awkward potion)

Potion of Leaping



Leaping potions allow you to jump higher for a short period of time. (Made with a rabbit's foot and an awkward potion)

Potion of Water Breathing



Water Breathing potions allow you to breath underwater for a short period of time. (Made with a pufferfish and an awkward potion)

Potion of Invisibility



Invisibility potions allow you to turn invisible for a short period of time. (Made with a fermented spider eye and potion of night vision)

Potion of Poison



Poison potions poison you for a short period of time. (Made with a spider eye and an awkward potion)

Potion of Weakness



Weakness potions weaken the attack damage you give for a short period of time. (Made with a fermented spider eye and a water bottle)

Potion of Slowness





Slowness potions make you walk and run slower for a short period of time. (Made with a fermented spider eye and a potion of fire resistance or a potion of swiftness)

Potion of Harming





Harming potions instantly harm you for a short period of time. (Made with a fermented spider eye and a potion of healing or a potion of poison)
