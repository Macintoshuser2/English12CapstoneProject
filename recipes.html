Golden Apple

A golden apple is used for a couple things. Most notably it is used to turn zombie villagers back into villagers when given to it after a potion of weakness. The recipe contains of 8 gold ingots and 1 apple

Andesite

Andesite is used for many different things, from building, to filling up holes. The recipe contains 1 block of andesite and one block of cobblestone.

Polished Andesite

Polished Andesite is used for many different things, from building, to filling up holes. The recipe contains 4 blocks of andesite.

Anvil

Anvils are used for repairing tools, armor, elytras, and renaming items and blocks. The recipe consists of 3 iron blocks and 4 ingots, which makes a grand total of 31 ingots per anvil.

Armor Stand

An armor stand can be used to put armor on display and is used in many redstone contraptions. The recipe consists of 6 sticks and 1 stone slab.

Arrow

Arrows are used with bows and dispensers. The recipe contains 1 flint, 1 stick, and 1 feather.

Spectral Arrow

Arrows are used with bows and dispensers. The recipe contains 1 flint, 1 stick, and 1 feather.
